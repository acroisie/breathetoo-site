BreatheToo — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

Overview

BreatheToo is a breathing and cold exposure training app developed by Arnaud Croisier. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

Account Data

Data When Storage
Apple ID identifier Sign in with Apple Device + iCloud
Name and email (optional) Only if you choose to share them Device + iCloud
Guest mode Continue as Guest No account data collected

Health Data

Data When Storage
Mindful minutes After each breathing session (if Apple Health enabled) Apple Health on device + iCloud
Cold exposure After each cold session (if Apple Health enabled) Apple Health on device + iCloud

We do not read any health data from other apps.

Session Data

Data When Storage
Breathing sessions After each session Device + iCloud
Cold exposure sessions After each session Device + iCloud
Session settings When you configure a session Device + iCloud

Session data includes duration, retention times, session type, water temperature, and user preferences.

Profile Data

Data When Storage
Profile photo (optional) When you add one Device + iCloud (compressed to under 500 KB)

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the app's core functionality (session tracking, history, statistics)
  • To sync your data across your Apple devices via iCloud
  • To display instructor sessions (fetched from our CloudKit public database)

We do not use your data for any other purpose.

Data Storage

All personal data is stored on your device and in your private iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit service.

What Where Who has access
Your personal data Your device + your iCloud account Only you
Instructor sessions CloudKit public database All users (read-only)
External servers None N/A

We do not operate any external servers. We do not have access to your personal data.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your personal data to any third party. We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Tracking

BreatheToo does not track you.

  • No device identifiers collected
  • No location data collected
  • No browsing history collected
  • No advertising identifiers used
  • No third-party SDKs for tracking or analytics

Your Rights

Right How to exercise
Delete local data Uninstall the app
Delete iCloud data Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Manage Storage > BreatheToo
Sign out Profile tab > Sign Out
Export health data Via Apple Health app
Disable health sharing Health app > Sharing > Apps > BreatheToo

For more details on managing your data, see our Privacy Choices page.

Children's Privacy

BreatheToo does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app does not contain age-restricted content.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL.
