Common questions

My sessions don't appear in Apple Health. Open BreatheToo → Profile → Settings and connect Apple Health. You can review or change the permission at any time in the Health app under Sharing → Apps → BreatheToo.

How do I delete my account and data? Profile → Account → Delete Account. This permanently removes your profile and session history from your device and iCloud.

Is BreatheToo free? Yes. No subscription, no ads.

Safety reminder Never practice breath retention in water or while driving. Read the in-app safety disclaimer before your first session, and consult a doctor if you have a medical condition.
