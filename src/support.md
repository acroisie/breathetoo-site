# BreatheToo — Support

Need help with BreatheToo? You're in the right place.

## Contact

- **Email**: [contact@breathetoo.com](mailto:contact@breathetoo.com)

We usually reply within a few days. Please include your iOS version and, if
relevant, the language your device is set to — it helps us reproduce issues
faster.

## Common questions

**My sessions don't appear in Apple Health.**
Open BreatheToo → Profile → Settings and connect Apple Health. You can review
or change the permission at any time in the Health app under
*Sharing → Apps → BreatheToo*.

**How do I delete my account and data?**
Profile → Account → Delete Account. This permanently removes your profile and
session history from your device and iCloud.

**Is BreatheToo free?**
Yes. No subscription, no ads.

**Safety reminder**
Never practice breath retention in water or while driving. Read the in-app
safety disclaimer before your first session, and consult a doctor if you have
a medical condition.
